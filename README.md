# Devcon 8 India — Mumbai Field Guide

An unofficial single-page city guide for attendees of **Devcon 8** in Mumbai, India (Nov 3–6, 2026, Jio World Convention Centre, BKC).

Covers getting to the venue, hotels, food filtered by diet, water & health, SIM/money, things to do, Web3-in-India, street smarts, and accessibility — with every place linked to Google Maps.

Static site (HTML/CSS/JS, no build step). Hosted with GitHub Pages.

> Unofficial and not affiliated with the Ethereum Foundation or Devcon. Verify dates, venue, visa rules, and prices on devcon.org.
